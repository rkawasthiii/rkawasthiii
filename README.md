### What I'm currently working on

**[Lead-Magnet](https://github.com/rkawasthiii/rkawasthiii-Lead-Magnet)** — Turn any landing page into a complete client acquisition package

Freelancers selling landing page rewrites spend 3-4 hours per prospect — auditing, scripting a Loom, writing cold DMs, handling objections, building the actual deliverable. LeadGen Machine compresses that into 90 seconds.

Paste a URL. The pipeline scrapes the page, scores it across 5 conversion dimensions, detects the business name, niche, city, and pain points — then generates a full conversion audit, rewritten landing page copy, a ready-to-use lead magnet prompt, and 9 personalized outreach scripts. Every output is baked with the business's context. Nothing generic.

Built for freelancers and agency owners who find prospects through Meta ads and want a repeatable system that scales without burning hours on prep.

`Next.js` `Supabase` `Amazon Bedrock` `Tailwind CSS` `Node.js`

**[Prana](https://github.com/rkawasthiii)** — Healthcare interoperability middleware *(Private)*

India's Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission requires every HMS platform to integrate with a national health network — patient identity verification, health record exchange, consent flows, ABDM gateway communication. Prana is the middleware layer that handles all of that, so hospitals don't have to build it themselves.

Plug in once. Become ABDM-compliant. No rebuilding core systems.

`Node.js` `Express` `MySQL` `ABDM APIs`
